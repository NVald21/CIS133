Selecting A Good Pet

  • If Possible Adopt

    • There are many dogs without a home, and adopting will help provide this.

  • Spend Some Time With The Dog

    • Don't just spend one hour with a dog. If you are thinking of choosing the dog, spend a few hours or more.

  • Don't Focus On Breed

    • We may have a breed in mind, but be open. The right dog will find you.

  • Try Being A Fostering A Dog

    • If you are unsure you can always foster a dog for a few nights or weeks.
