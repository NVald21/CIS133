Finding your sound is just as important as finding your look. Drum shells come in a variety of wood types like maple, ash, oak, among many others.
Choose yours for the right sound today.

  • Ash: Throaty and warm. Pronounced warm highs and mids, average low end.
  • Birch: Loud and cutting. High frequencies, reduced mid-range, good low end punch.
  • Mahogany: Vibrant and resonant. Muted highs, smooth midrange, rich low end.
  • Maple: All purpose. Even amount of high and mid tones
  • Oak: All purpose. Soft highs, good midrange, warm lows.
  • Walnut: Big and warm. Equal highs, mids, and lows.
